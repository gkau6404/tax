How does it work?

If you earned $66,000 or less last tax year, the City can help you file your taxes for free. You can file online or, if you make under $54,000, you can file in person.

Documents

Gather Your Tax Documents. W-2s, 1099s, Mortgage interest statements, Investment income statements.

Organize

Organize Paperwork. Purchase a few manila folders, an accordion file or a filing system that will hold your tax documents.

File

File Your Taxes.According to a 2017 survey, most Americans chose to either hire a professional (32%) or use commercial tax software (35%) when they filed the previous year.

Relax

Your taxes have been filed and you no longer have to worry about missing that April deadline.
